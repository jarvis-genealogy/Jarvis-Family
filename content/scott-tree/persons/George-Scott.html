George SCOTT

Death date: 6 November 1876
Death place: Adelaide, Adelaide City, South Australia, Australia
Married: Sarah Bryant 8 Apr 1828 Fersfield, England


Details

The record of Neil Quintrell lists only George's birth date as 1812 in Norfolk and the 1841 Census estimates about 1811, whereas FamilySearch and Findagrave list it more precisely as 17 December 1807, South Norfolk District, Norfolk, England and so this is taken as correct.

Neil's record states that George and Sarah Scott arrived at Port Adelaide on the 'Caucasian' on 6 (or 8) Feb 1852 with their five children.

Elizabeth 1838 - 1892 married Thomas HENRY
Frederick 1840 - 1890 married Elizabeth BROWN
Julia 1845 married Arthur Medd
Charles Sidney 1848 - 1912 married Ellen GREEN
Clara 1850

George Scott's occupation was recorded as 'agricultural labourer''.

FamilySearch shows 7 children
Julia Scott 1830
George Frederick Scott 1842
Charles Sidney Scott 1849-1910
Clair Clement Scott 1849-1910
Arthur
Elizabeth
Julia MEDD

We can see that Julia Medd and Arthur (Medd) are probably incorrectly listed and that Neil's work is most likely correct, but that Clara is Clair Clement Scott as confirmed by Findagrave.
