Sarah BRYANT


Birth date: 3 December 1809
Birth place: Norfolk, England, United Kingdom
Death date: 19 March 1896
Death place: Adelaide, South Australia, Australia
Married: George Scott 8 Apr 1828 Fersfield, England


Details

From Findagrave :
The daughter of Henry Bryant and Sarah Wright. She married George Scott in 1828. The 1841 and 1851 English Census shows her living in Thelnetham and Redgrave. It was while in the Redgrave Common she and her husband decided to emigrate to South Australia arriving on the vessel Caucasian with their five children in 1852.
